My Story

Local Expertise.
Global Connections.

Lorenzo Scartozzi is a distinguished real estate professional renowned for infusing passion and charisma into every property transaction. With an innate ability to secure premium outcomes, Lorenzo is your key to unlocking exceptional results in the Waikato market.

Originally from Italy, Lorenzo has seamlessly woven his life into the fabric of Waikato. His unique background in the equestrian industry — managing stallions across the region — enhances his understanding of the local landscape and adds an extra layer of expertise to his real estate ventures.

Lorenzo's love for Waikato stems from its expansive green spaces, tranquil surroundings, and the warmth of its people. His meticulous attention to detail and strong management skills set him apart, ensuring success without compromise.
